Crispy Chicken-fry


Chicken is no doubt the most popular poultry meat on the planet, which is popular because of its unique taste, flavour and texture.
It is one of the few meat varieties that can be cooked in any way like grilling, frying, roasting, steaming and more.
This makes it the perfect meat for snacks and full meals.
One of the best chicken snacks that can also be eaten as a dinner meal is Kodi Vepudu.
Kodi Vepudu is an Indian dish, which includes fried chicken in a vegetable tadka.
It is highly nutritious as chicken is a good source of proteins, vitamin B12, phosphorus, vitamin B6 and nutrients.
So, to surprise your family, prepare this wonderful chicken dish at home with several varieties like Guntur Kodi Vepudu and Miriyala Kodi Vepudu.
